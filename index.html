Gift Registry

Your presence is the greatest gift of all, but if you'd like to contribute to our next chapter, we've created a registry with some items we'd love. Here are some instructions on how to use the registry site:

  • Click the button below to visit our registry and browse through the items we've selected. You’re welcome to team up with others to purchase a gift together!
  • Click on the items that you are interested in to see more details. If they've already been reserved, feel free to contact the reserver(s) to discuss sharing the expense.
  • Once you’ve decided on an item, make sure to Reserve it. You’ll be prompted to provide your name and email so the site can keep track of your selection.
  • Changed your mind? No problem! Select the item you reserved and click Release to make it available for others again.
  • When you’re certain about your purchase, click Purchase to notify others that the item is no longer available.
  • If you have any questions or need assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out!
